Chocolate Cake

Prep Time 45 min
Servings 8 slices
Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder (unsweetened)
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add buttermilk, oil, eggs, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool completely before slicing. Enjoy!
